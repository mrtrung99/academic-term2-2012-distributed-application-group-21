Opening The Restaurant New Branch02 May 2012

Mari Vanna, a Russian restaurant and bar, is now open in Knightsbridges
It will follow in the footsteps of the original in St Petersburg, which is named after a mythical Russian matriarch who invited weary travellers into her home to be fed. The restaurant's old-world interior features a traditional Russian Pechka oven and shelves filled with dolls and trinkets. The menu includes traditional borscht, pelmeni (dumplings), stuffed cabbage leaves, and pirozhki (Russian pies).
